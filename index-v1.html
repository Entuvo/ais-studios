Process

How it works

A streamlined workflow built around your creative vision.

01

Consultation

We start with a conversation. Share your references, your vision, and what you want the music to feel like. No jargon, just honest dialogue about your sound.

02

Pre-Production

Song structure, arrangement, and sonic direction get mapped out. If you need beats or instrumentation, this is where the programming begins.

03

Recording & Production

The studio doors open. Vocals are tracked, instruments are layered, and the raw elements of your track come to life in a focused, creative environment.

04

Mix & Master

Every frequency finds its place. The mix is sculpted for impact and the master is tuned for distribution. You receive files ready for release.
